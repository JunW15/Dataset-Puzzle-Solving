1. @Summary
Sum the Skyline!
1. Description
Please play ‘Skyscrapers’ first, as this is a variation of that game.
All the rules about ‘Skyscrapers’ are valid, but the numbers you see on the borders tell you the SUM of the heights of the Skyscrapers you see.
1. Tips
1. In the example, the 10 must be the sum of 1,2,3 and 4 – in that order.
2. And remember you’re ALWAYS seeing the highest (4), so it’s always included in the sum. So, when the sum is 4..
