Fishing Neighbours
1. Description
The board represents a piece of antartic, which some fishermen want to split in equal parts.
They decide each one should have a piece of land of exactly 4 squares, including one fishing hole.
Divide the land accordingly.
Ice blocks are just obstacles and don’t count as piece of land.
1. Tips
1. The puzzle is usually not terribly hard, but enjoyable. If you mess up, try starting over from a different angle.
