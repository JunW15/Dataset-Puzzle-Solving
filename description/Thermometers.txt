1. @Summary
Puzzle Fever
1. Description
On the board a few Thermometers are laid down. Your goal is to fill them according to the hints.
In a Thermometer, mercury always starts at the bulb and can progressively fill the Thermometer towards the end.
A Thermometer can also be completely empty, including the bulb.
The numbers on the border tell you how many filled sections are present on that on that Row or Column.
1. Tips
1. First, check large numbers, or zeros
2. Thermometers orientation matters a lot.
