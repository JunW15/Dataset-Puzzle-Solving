1. @Summary
Each Arrow points to a Star and every Star has an arrow pointing at it.
1. Description
In the board you have to find the hidden Stars.
Each Star is pointed at by at least one Arrow and each Arrow points to at least one Star.
The numbers on the border tells you how many Stars there are in that row or column.
1. Variant
Some level have a variation of these rules: Stars must be pointed by one and only one Arrow.
In these levels you’ll see a notice in the top right corner.
1. Tips
1. Remember that if a tile has no Arrow pointing at it, it must be empty.
2. At the same time an Arrow must have at least one Star in its path, but not necessarily just one.
