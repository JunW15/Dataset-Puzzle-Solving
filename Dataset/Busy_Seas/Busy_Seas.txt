1.@Summary
More seafaring puzzles
2. Description
Similar to Lighthouses, you're still at sea and you need to find the lighthouses that light the boats.
Each boat has a number on it that tells you how many Lighthouses are lighting it.
A Lighthouse lights all the tiles horizontally and vertically.
Differently than 'Lighthouses', its light is stopped by the first boat it meets.
A lighthouse lights at least on boat.
Differently than 'Lighthouses', lighthouses can touch boats and other Lighthouses.
3. Tips
Remember Lightouses don't block themselves.
Harder than it looks. Try Lighthouses first.
