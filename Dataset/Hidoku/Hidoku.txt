1. @Summary
Jump from one neighboring tile to another and fill the board.
2. Description
Starting at tile number 1, reach the last tile (16 in the example) by jumping from tile to tile.
When jumping from a tile, you can land on any tile around it, horizontally, vertically or diagonally touching.
The goal is to jump on every tile, only once and reach the last tile.
3. Tips
a. You can note down sub-paths, even if it's not linked to the tiles you already discovered.
