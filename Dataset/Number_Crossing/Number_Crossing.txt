1. @Summary
Digital Crosswords
2. Description
Find the numbers in the board.
Numbers connot touch each other, not even diagonally.
On the top ad left of the grid, you're given how many numbers are in that column or row.
On the bottom and right of the grid, you're given the sum of the numbers on that column or row.
3. Tips
a. Try to eliminate sums that cannot be reached with some low numbers.
b. Or single numbers that are bigger than a sum.
