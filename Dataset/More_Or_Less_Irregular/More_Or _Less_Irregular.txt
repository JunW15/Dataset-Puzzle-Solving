1. @Summary
Mr. Futoshiki, meet Mr. Sudoku.
1. Description
More Or Less can be seen as a mix between Sudoku and Futoshiki.
One of the Standard Sudoku rules apply
	a.	one number only can appear once in every row and column.
	b.	Instead of the classic 3x3 Sudoku, the game have irregular areas, one number only can appear once in one region.
The hints however, are given by the Greater Than or Less Than symbols between tiles.

1. Tips
1. You might want to use Notes quite often.
2. Start with lowest (1,2) and highest (9,8) numbers, then work towards the middle.
