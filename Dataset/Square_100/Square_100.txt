1. @Summary
Exactly one hundred.
2. Description
You are given a 3x3 or 4x4 square with digits in it.
You have to add digits to some (or all) tiles, in order to produce the sum of 100 for every row and column.
You can add digits before or after the given one.
3. Tips
a. Harder than it looks.
b. Remember that especially on the 4x4 you won't always have to add digits to all cells.
