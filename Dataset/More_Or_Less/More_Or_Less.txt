1. @Summary
Mr. Futoshiki, meet Mr. Sudoku.
2. Description
More Or Less can be seen as a mix between Sudoku and Futoshiki.
Standard Sudoku rules apply
	a.	one number only can appear once in every row and column.
	b.	one number only can appear once in 3*3 sub area.
The hints however, are given by the Greater Than or Less Than symbols between tiles.
3. Tips
a. You might want to use Notes quite often.
b. Start with lowest (1,2) and highest (9,8) numbers, then work towards the middle.
