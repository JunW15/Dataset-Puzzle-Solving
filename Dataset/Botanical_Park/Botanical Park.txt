1. @Summary
Excuse me sir? Do you know where the Harpagophytum Procumbens is?
2. Description
The board represent a Botanical Park, with arrows pointing to the different plants.
Each arrow points to at least one plant and there is exactly a plant in every row and in every column.
Plants cannot touch, not even diagonally.
3. Important
Puzzles with side 9 or bigger have TWO trees in every row and column. 
4. Tips
a. Try Parks and Hidden Starts first.
b. Look for tight spots.
