1. @Summary
Each Arrow points to a Star and every Star has an arrow pointing at it.
2. Description
In the board you have to find the hidden Stars.
Each Star is pointed at by at least one Arrow and each Arrow points to at least one Star.
The numbers on the border tells you how many Stars there are in that row or column.
3. Tips
a. Remember that if a tile has no Arrow pointing at it, it must be empty.
b. At the same time an Arrow must have at least one Star in its path, but not necessarily just one.
